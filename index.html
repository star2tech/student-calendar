My story

I built the planner
I wished existed.

I'm Hiwot Tsegaye, a 14-year-old web developer. StudentCal started because I was genuinely struggling to keep up with all my schoolwork — assignments were piling up, deadlines were sneaking up on me, and I kept doing everything last minute.

I looked for something to help, but every decent planner app wanted you to pay for it. That felt wrong to me. Students are already stressed enough — they shouldn't have to spend money just to stay organized.

So I built my own. It's free, it works in your browser, and you can just tell it what you need in plain English. No subscriptions, no sign-ups, no nonsense. Just you and your calendar.

What I believe in
